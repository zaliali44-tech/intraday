Control Inputs

Hybrid backlog design:
In simple mode, backlog is entered once as a day-level control input and distributed into the risk logic. In interval mode, backlog becomes editable in the interval grid for teams that have detailed tracking.

Formula Glossary

Forecast Accuracy %

100 − (|Actual Volume − Forecast Volume| ÷ Forecast Volume × 100)
Volume Variance %

(Actual Volume − Forecast Volume) ÷ Forecast Volume × 100
AHT Variance %

(Actual AHT − Target AHT) ÷ Target AHT × 100
Staffing Gap

Available HC − Required HC
Occupancy %

(Required HC ÷ Available HC) × 100
Risk Score

Weighted score based on volume variance, AHT variance, staffing gap, SLA miss, backlog pressure, and occupancy pressure.
